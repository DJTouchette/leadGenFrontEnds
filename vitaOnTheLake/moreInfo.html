Developer

CentreCourt Developments is focused on the execution of well thought out high-rise condominiums located in close proximity to all the major amenities of Toronto, rapid transit networks and employment areas. CentreCourt believes that this approach to development will contribute to Toronto’s vibrancy, sustainability and competitiveness.

CentreCourt currently has five residential condominium projects in development throughout downtown Toronto, collectively representing over 2,600 homes and over 1 billion dollars of development value. CentreCourt strives to exceed customer expectations and to be a leader in the real estate development industry. CentreCourt’s developments are collaborative endeavors undertaken with best-in-class planners, architects, interior designers and construction trades in order to ensure that the homes CentreCourt develops are of the highest quality standard. CentreCourt was behind the most successful condo launches of 2011, 2012, 2014, and 2015 with Peter Street Condos, Karma Condos, INDX Condos, Core Condos and Grid Condos.

Architect

Founded in 1926, Page + Steele Architects is an international, award-winning firm, with a tradition of design excellence. The firm recently merged with IBI Group and is now known as Page + Steele IBI Group Architects. P+S IBI has extensive expertise designing residential, hotel, office, retail, and mixed-use buildings. Some of the firm’s recent work includes L’Avenue in Montreal and Delta’s new flagship hotel in downtown Toronto, which won the City of Toronto’s 2015 Urban Design Award of Excellence for tall buildings.

P+S IBI has also designed many of Toronto’s exclusive, high-end residential developments such as The Ritz Carlton Hotel & Residences (in association with KPF), The Hazelton Hotel and Residences, and Museum House. Most recently P+S IBI designed the highly successful and architecturally acclaimed Core Condos, INDX Condos and Grid Condos for CentreCourt. Axis Condos is the fourth project P+S IBI has designed for CentreCourt.
