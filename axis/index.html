Axis Condos Overview

Axis is a 38 storey tower that includes a 6 storey podium made to showcase the balance of life and work in one. Distinct in design, it will become a signature piece of the Toronto skyline. With a honeycomb-like pattern gracing the façade, it’s design speaks to the idea of community while staying chic, unique and sophisticated. The end result is a look that invites you in to experience life at Axis – one of perfect harmony.

Lobby. Dressed
in Fendi.

From the moment you step into the lobby, cool contemporary luxury sets the tone for life here. Relaxed in feel, the area is dressed in clean white with touches of gold throughout, which complement the Fendi furnishings beautifully. Though the art in the entrance creates impact and holds significant presence, the rest of the space is airy, inspired and most of all, welcoming.

Fitness and Shared WorkSpace.

The amenities quickly turn into more functional environments as you move into Axis Shared Workspace. As the very first condo in Toronto to give residents a place to collaborate where they live, Axis gives its residents a mix of private meeting corners and large open areas. From end to end, this zone lets you work for yourself, but not by yourself. Inspired by the work environments at Google and Facebook, the energy of the environment fosters leadership through the power of community.

Want to unleash more energy? Take it to the fitness area. With approximately 6,500 sq. ft. of space, it uses the idea of community as inspiration and motivation. Residents can find top-notch equipment, a steam room, wide open space and people who help them push themselves. There’s no need to go far to get the very best workout. Everything is right where you live, designed to bring out your very best.

Teslas Included.

Built for urban life, Axis is the first condo community to have two Teslas available exclusively for residents. Whether it’s carpooling, running some errands or hitting the town, these rides will make the most of every moment on the road.
